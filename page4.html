About Me

My name is Brody Billings, I'am currently 25 and enrolled at Fanshawe College for Interactive Media Design. The theme I selected for this website was my personal shoe and clothing collection. I selected this theme as it is something I have been into for a while now. As I was growing up I started to develop a liking for for the more expensive sneakers and clothing which at the time would have been around that $150 for a pair of shoes, now turning into the collection I have today. The first "hype" shoe purchased was a pair of Yeezy 350 V2 Semi-Frozen Yellow which I was lucky enough to get on launch. Anytime after that when I saw a sneaker that I liked was launching I would test my luck. There are many sneakers that would fit into my dream sneakers category from a Nike collab with popular name Dior with the Jordan 1 High Diors, the Freddy Kruger's or the "Chunky Dunky's" one of the latest and biggest collabs with Ben and Jerry's.
